These are the sonic DNA for our theme. Feed into prompts as style/mood references.

## Generation Strategy

1. **Generate all 6 main theme prompts** (A-F) via Lyria 2 in one batch
2. **Michael picks 1-2 winners** — these become the DNA for all other themes
3. **Re-generate winners with slight prompt variations** (tempo, key mood, instrumentation tweaks) for variety
4. **Generate 5 secondary themes** referencing the winning style
5. **Generate 2 ambient texture layers** for under-music depth
6. **Total cost:** ~$0.52 for all 13 clips ($0.04 × 13)

### Technical notes
- All clips are 48kHz WAV, ~6.3MB each → convert to MP3 (128kbps) = ~500KB each
- Seamless loop: need to trim start/end to match zero-crossings — manual step
- Layer mixing: ambient at 15%, music at 40%, SFX at 70% in-game via Web Audio gain nodes
