# Sub-Level Environmental Variations — Darius Star

## Concept
Each of the 10 biomes has 10 sub-levels (1-1 through 10-10). Within each biome, the environment gets progressive variations — same core theme, different flavor. This keeps gameplay visually fresh across 100 levels.

## Variation Types Per Biome
Each biome gets 5 variation types, cycled across its 10 sub-levels:
- **A: Light/Shallow** (levels 1-2) — brighter, easier, welcoming
- **B: Core/Standard** (levels 3-4) — the iconic biome look
- **C: Dark/Deep** (levels 5-6) — deeper, more dangerous
- **D: Corrupted** (levels 7-8) — taken over by the enemy, darker palette
- **E: Extreme/Boss** (levels 9-10) — dramatic, end-of-biome intensity
